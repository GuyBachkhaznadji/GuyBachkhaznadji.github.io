Search Buddy is a unique tailored tool to help you search for candidates.

All you need to do is insert the relevant job title and role profile and Search Buddy will do the rest.

Search Buddy will produce a search based on the key words in the role profile that relate to that job title to do 3 things:

  • Give you a good starting point for your search.
  • Identify most of the key words in that role profile that are relevant to this job.
  • Produce a usable search to find the best candidates.
